# 🎯 Gameplay Explanation

## Objective

The goal of the game is to find and shoot all **25 hidden targets** within the time limit.

## Time System

* Total in-game time: **25,000 seconds**
* Time scaling: **1 in-game second = 0.01 real seconds**
* Actual gameplay time: **~250 seconds**

## Game Flow

1. Player spawns in a large open-world map.
2. Targets are placed across different areas of the map.
3. Player explores the environment to locate targets.
4. Shooting a target marks it as completed.
5. Game ends when:

   * All targets are found, OR
   * Time runs out

## Challenge

* Large map size increases difficulty
* Targets are hidden in various locations
* Limited time forces quick decision-making
